Teaching

While my INSERM position does not formally require teaching duties, I am passionate about education and helping the next generation of scientists. I voluntarily engage in teaching activities in several forms, from course coordination to invited lectures and summer schools, because I believe in the importance of sharing knowledge and fostering scientific curiosity.
